José São João

I am a mathematics doctoral student at University of Strasbourg. I am in the Algebra, Representation, Topology (ART) team in IRMA. My supervisors are Vladimir Dotsenko and Najib Idrissi.

Academic path

  • Undergratuate studies was completed at the University of Johannesburg supervised by Amartya Goswami (2017-2020).
  • My Masters degree was obtain from Stockholm University and KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Sweden (2021-2023).
    • My master thesis, "Koszulness of Torelli Lie algebras", was supervised by Alexander Berglund.

Research

My research interests are algebaric operads, homological algebra, algebraic topology and combinatorics.
